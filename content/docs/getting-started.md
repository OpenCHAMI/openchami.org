Welcome to OpenCHAMI! Here's how to get started and stay connected with the community.

## Join Our Community

### Communication Channels

**Slack** – Connect with community members in real-time
- [Join our Slack workspace](https://join.slack.com/t/openchami/shared_invite/zt-2xn9wctqq-tptRqPUeFQtTsENRkrCkBg)

**Mailing List** – Subscribe for announcements and discussions
- [OpenCHAMI Members Mailing List](https://lists.hpsf.io/g/openchami-members)

### Meetings & Office Hours

Join us for regular Technical Steering Committee (TSC) meetings and office hours:
- [View our public calendar](https://zoom-lfx.platform.linuxfoundation.org/meetings/openchami)

## Development & Contributions

### GitHub

**Main Project** – Explore OpenCHAMI repositories and code
- [OpenCHAMI on GitHub](https://github.com/openchami)

**Community Repo** – Questions about governance and project direction
- [OpenCHAMI Community Repository](https://github.com/openchami/community)

### Roadmap & RFDs

**RFDs in Progress** – Request for Discussion (RFD) documents showing planned features and improvements
- [View open RFDs](https://github.com/OpenCHAMI/roadmap/issues?q=is%3Aissue%20state%3Aopen%20label%3Arfd)

## Next Steps

1. **Join Slack** and introduce yourself to the community
2. **Subscribe to the mailing list** to stay informed about updates
3. **Check the calendar** for upcoming office hours and meetings
4. **Explore the GitHub repositories** to understand the codebase
5. **Review open RFDs** to learn about upcoming features and contribute ideas

Have questions? Feel free to reach out on Slack or email the mailing list!
