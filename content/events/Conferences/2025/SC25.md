**Event**: SC25 – The International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage, and Analysis

**Date**: November 16–21, 2025

**Location**: America's Center, St. Louis, MO

**Website**: [SC25 Conference](https://sc25.supercomputing.org/)

**Registration**: [Register for SC25](https://sc25.supercomputing.org/registration/)

SC25 is the premier international conference for high performance computing, networking, storage, and analysis. OpenCHAMI will be featured in two Birds of a Feather (BoF) sessions:

## OpenCHAMI Participation

### High Performance Software Foundation BoF
**Session**: [High Performance Software Foundation BoF](https://sc25.conference-program.com/presentation/?id=bof249&sess=sess421)  
**Time**: Tuesday, November 18, 2025, 12:15pm – 1:15pm CST  
**Location**: Room 230

Join the HPSF BoF to learn about developments over the past year, including updates on CI/CD, Benchmarking, and Binary Packaging working groups, news from member projects including OpenCHAMI, and new outreach activities.

### OpenHPC BoF
**Session**: [OpenHPC and the Future of Open-Source HPC Provisioning Featuring Warewulf, Confluent, and OpenCHAMI](https://sc25.conference-program.com/presentation/?id=bof177&sess=sess481)  
**Time**: Thursday, November 20, 2025, 12:15pm – 1:15pm CST  
**Location**: Room 230

This BoF showcases leading open-source provisioning platforms with lightning talks from the Warewulf, Confluent, and OpenCHAMI communities. The session highlights recent advances in container-based provisioning, cloud-native HPC management, and security-focused deployment strategies. **Travis Cotton** will represent OpenCHAMI.

Read more about OpenCHAMI [here](/docs/introduction-to-openchami/) and try it yourself through the [install guide](/guides/getting_started/).
