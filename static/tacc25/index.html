TACC 25 – OpenCHAMI Developer Summit

September 9–11, 2025 · Texas Advanced Computing Center, Austin, TX

Details Location & Directions Schedule Tracks & Chairs Registration Sponsors

Event Details

The OpenCHAMI community is gathering at the Texas Advanced Computing Center (TACC) for three days of collaboration, learning and hacking. Whether you’re new to OpenCHAMI or a long‑time contributor, there will be tracks for every skill level. Meet fellow sysadmins, developers and researchers working on open HPC automation and help shape the project’s future.

Hosts: TACC & The OpenCHAMI Community.

Location & Directions

Venue: Advanced Computing Building (ACB), Texas Advanced Computing Center, 10505 Exploration Way, Austin, TX 78758.

Please note that the TACC address is 10100 Burnet Rd, Austin, TX 78758 , however the entrance is on Exploration Way.

From the Burnet Road entrance, continue past the guard booth (you don’t need to stop), pass the chain link gate to Exploration Way, then make a right. ACB is the second to last building on the right with the overhanging patios. From the Braker Lane entrance, ACB is the second building on the left with the overhanging patios (10505 Exploration Way).

When you arrive at ACB, you may park in the covered parking lot or on the street. Please obtain a parking pass from one of the kiosks, or you may use the ParkMobile app (zone 96113). Please check in with the receptionist when you enter the building.
